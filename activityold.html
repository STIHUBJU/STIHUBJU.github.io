Activities

Mask Distribution

On May 5, 2020 a workshop was conducted for hundred beneficiaries from Goner village where they were given the mask to protect themselves from the coronavirus infection and were given more information about the prevention of the virus.

Kadha Distribution

STI HUB JU has distributed Kadha material to the rural people of Goner village where around 150 people were present and awareness program for the people about the prevention from the virus was conducted.

Mask Preparation

STI Hub, JECRC University organized the activity where villagers from Vidhani were taught about the mask preparation. Mr. Ram Ratan has taught the villagers how to prepare the mask. The workshop was conducted from 16 November to 20 November 2020.

Jewellery Preparation

Villagers from Vidhani village were taught about the Jewellery preparation under the auspicious banner of STI hub JECRC University from 7th December to 12 December 2020 where the resource person Mr. Asit has taught them about jewellery making using wire.

Jewellery Preparation using wires

The STI Hub has organized a workshop for the people from the Vidhani about making jewelry using wire and then folding and molding them to make beautiful jewelry. The students actively participated and made jewelry out of them.

Pamphlets Distribution

Science technology and innovation hub JECRC University went to the village where they have distributed the pamphlet to the 35 rural people of Vidhani village on 1st February 2021 and informed the people about the STI activities and asked people to join and participated in the activities conducted by STI Hub, JECRC University, Jaipur.

Interaction with agricultural expert

On 2nd February 2021, the interaction with the agricultural expert was conducted where Professor RK Bansal has brief the 5 beneficiary from Agriculture Department JECRC University about the new agriculture Technology.

Interaction with government school

Science technology and innovation hub has interacted with the principal of government school with on February 3rd 2021 with 15 beneficiaries including staff members of the school and the children were part of the workshop too.

Interaction with villagers

On 4th February 2021, the STI team member had interaction with the Villagers of Goner where around 18 people were present there, the Science technology and innovation members had distributed the pamphlet and discussed about the activities which would be conducted under the banner of STI including mask stitching, Jewelry preparation and other.

Medical plant collection

On 5 February 2021, a workshop of medicinal plant collection from Goshala Jaipur was organized where resource person Mr. R.P Jain and Dr. Shilpa were present with five beneficiaries.

Potli Making Activity

JECRC University with the banner of STI hub has conducted a workshop of Potali making activity for the rural people of Vidhani village on 6th February 2021 where 11 beneficiaries present there. The rural people were told the process of making Potali. They were also provided with all the materials.

Significance of aloe Vera plantation

Science technology and innovation hub JU has organized a workshop of Professor RK Bansal, he has taught the rural people of Vidhani village about the significance of Aloe Vera plantation on 8 February 2021, the villagers were taught that how they can grow Aloevera plant using very less amount which would give them huge returns in the future with all the benefits.

Scarf making activity

On the February 9, 2021 a workshop was organized under the science technology and innovation hub of JECRC University where the villagers were given the training of Scarf making. They were provided with all the required materials. They were told the process of drawing first with chalk and then keeping two cloths upon one another and then work.

Jewellery making activity

The Villagers of Saligrampura came to JECRC University and they were taught to make wire Jewellery using copper and brass wire, they were given all the necessary things required to make jewellery. Also they were brief about how they can sell their jewellery through an online and offline mode. They were very keen to learn this process where they created the design on paper, then they need to twist the wire with round and flat Pliers for shape according to the design and then they can add any color stone for enhance the creative of product.

Basic Computer Training

On February 19, 2021 a workshop of Computer training for preparing Word document with rural people was conducted for 17 beneficiaries under Dr. Anju kalavar.

3d printer Workshop

Students from design department were taught the usage of 3D printer machine for Installation technique for jewelry casting machine on February 22, 2021

workshop

Science technology innovation hub JECRC University has organized a workshop on February 22, 2021 of installation techniques for casting machine for 10 beneficiaries, where the villagers were taught the usage of these machinery and were told about their benefits.

workshop

STI HUB JECRC University Jaipur organized the four day workshop where villagers from Saligrampura and Chaksu came to the university where they learned about the important ingredients and the process of the herbal tie and dye, how can they use the materials which are easily available in their house and can do this process of herbal tie and dye.
